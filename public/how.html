How It Works

  1. Step 1 — Register / Login

    Create your account and set up your profile.

  2. Step 2 — Choose Your Path

    Post a new project (with description, skills required, and payment/revenue model) or join an existing project as a contributor.

  3. Step 3 — Collaborate

    Work in teams of 4–5 members, supported by our community tools.

  4. Step 4 — Grow Together

    Project creators pay/share revenue as promised. Contributors earn certificates, badges, and real-world experience.
